ToDo Task Tracking

It's all about 2 not 3 prioritization

This app tracks ToDo tasks by Date, Category and Status.

The objective is to help prioritize tasks daily by displaying the whole list.

The Prioritize button will go thru all the list starting with the top 3. The app will ask you to choose 2 (ie. eliminate 1). It will then continue with the next item on the list.

The top 2 go to the top of the list.

The set of priorities can be altered to prioritize top N (max 5). In addition to eliminating tasks to prioritize, you can commit to a task during the priotization process.
